About us

At our cricket academy, we are dedicated to providing the best possible cricket coaching to aspiring cricketers of all ages and abilities. Our team of experienced and certified coaches are passionate about the sport and committed to helping each and every student achieve their goals.


Our facilities are state-of-the-art and include indoor and outdoor practice nets, as well as a fully-equipped gym and classroom for theory sessions. We believe that a well-rounded approach to cricket coaching is essential, which is why we place great emphasis on physical fitness, mental preparation, and tactical understanding of the game.

The role of a cricket coach is not limited to training players on the field. They are responsible for creating a team culture that fosters discipline, respect, and accountability. Our coaches identify the strengths and weaknesses of each player and devise strategies to improve their overall performance.


Our coaching staff is comprised of experienced and certified coaches who are passionate about the sport and committed to helping each and every student achieve their goals. Our coaches are dedicated to providing the best possible cricket coaching to aspiring cricketers of all ages and abilities.


At our cricket academy, we are committed to creating a positive and supportive learning environment where our students can thrive and achieve their full potential. We believe that cricket is not just a sport, but a way of life, and we are proud to be a part of our students' journey towards becoming successful cricketers.

We offer a range of programs to suit the needs of every student, from group coaching sessions to one-on-one coaching, as well as specialized programs for specific skill sets such as batting, bowling, and wicketkeeping. Our coaching methodology is tailored to each student's individual needs, ensuring that they receive personalized attention and guidance.


In addition to our coaching programs, we also organize regular tournaments and matches, giving our students the opportunity to put their skills into practice and compete against other teams. We believe that this competitive experience is an important part of a student's development as a cricketer, as it allows them to test their abilities and learn from their mistakes.

Facilities

Our academy is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to ensure that our students have the best possible experience.

  • Full-size Ground with Turf Wickets
  • BCCI and NIS certified coaches
  • 2 set of Outdoor Nets with Turf, Astro and Cemented Wickets
  • Indoor Nets with Astro and turf wickets
  • A fully equipped gym
  • Tours, Tournaments and visits by former players
  • Hostel, Dining Hall
  • Video Analysis, Bowling Machine & Floodlights
