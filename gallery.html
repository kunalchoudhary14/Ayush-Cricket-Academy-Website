Ayush Cricket Academy

Center of Excellence

Gallery

We are a premier cricket academy dedicated to developing the next generation of cricket stars. Our academy offers world-class coaching programs designed to provide our students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed at all levels of the game. Our team of experienced coaches is passionate about cricket and committed to helping our students reach their full potential. At our academy, we believe that cricket is more than just a game. It's an opportunity to learn valuable life skills, such as teamwork, sportsmanship, leadership, and perseverance. We are proud to be part of a sport that promotes healthy competition, respect for others, and a strong sense of community.

Facilities

Our academy is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to ensure that our students have the best possible experience.

  • Full-size Ground with Turf Wickets
  • BCCI and NIS certified coaches
  • 2 set of Outdoor Nets with Turf, Astro and Cemented Wickets
  • Indoor Nets with Astro and turf wickets
  • A fully equipped gym
  • Tours, Tournaments and visits by former players
  • Hostel, Dining Hall
  • Video Analysis, Bowling Machine & Floodlights
